Autonomy Talks

Autonomy Talks are open to everyone and no registration is required. The goal of the talks is the exchange among different groups, inside and outside ETH Zurich, working on innovations in Robotics and Control. Autonomy Talks will take place virtually, on Zoom. Check out the official webpage for the details.
