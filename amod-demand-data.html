Demand Datasets for AMoD

In the following we report a list of demand datasets, useful when performing simulations of Autonomous Mobility-on-Demand Systems (AMoD).

  1. The well-known New York City (USA) Taxi dataset;
  2. The recent Chicago (USA) dataset (including ride-hailing apps data);
  3. The Washington D.C. (USA) Taxi dataset;
  4. The San Francisco (USA) dataset;
  5. The Boston (USA) dataset (including ride-hailing apps data);
  6. The Berlin (GE) dataset;
  7. The datasets provided by MatSim, which include the following cities: Caracas (Venezuela), Aliaga, Izmir (Turkey), Joinville (Brazil), Padang (Indonesia), Gauteng (South Africa), Singapore, Seoul (South Korea), Quito (Ecuador), Vorarlberg (Austria), Poznan (Poland), Paris (France), Dakar (Senegal), Munich (Germany), Zurich (Switzerland), Tel Aviv (Israel), Toronto (Canada), Sioux Falls (USA);
  8. The San Francisco (USA) dataset;
  9. The DiDi Chengdu (China) dataset;
  10. The Tokyo (Japan) dataset;
Please e-mail gzardini@ethz.ch if you are aware of other (publicly available) demand datasets for AMoD systems.
